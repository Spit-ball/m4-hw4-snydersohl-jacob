About Dax

>>>>>>> 862510b319a28bfe1e9063b1aad5d25bb5f27e75 Dax, or Daniel Nwosu Jr., is a rap artist, singer, and songwriter from Canada. Dax, also often stylized in all caps as DAX, was born March 22, 1994. In his early life, Dax moved from Ottawa to Wichita, Kansas to attend college, before ending up in Los Angeles, California as a student at Newman University. During his time at Newman, he played guard for the Newman Jets -- a collegiate basketball team, and kicked off his rap career while working as a janitor for the university.

The Rise of Dax

>>>>>>> 862510b319a28bfe1e9063b1aad5d25bb5f27e75 In 2016, during a bus ride alongside his basketball team, Dax was working on writing poetry; his friends were quite impressed by his ability. Leveraging popular music app "SoundCloud" as a way to broadcast his music, he began turning his poetry into songs. His initial rise to fame can be attributed to the official music video produced for his song 'Catch Me Outside', featuring Danielle Bregoli. Later, Dax would release his first extended play, 'It's Different Now', in August 2018, which was supported by guests such as OT Genasis and Futuristic.
